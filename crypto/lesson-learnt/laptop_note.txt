Blog idea - how to tame a bear recipe (draft):  
This is no easy task. Taming a bear can be rough. Now, the first thing you want to do is cut an apple into halves.  
Then add a dash potion mixer to the cauldron. Make sure to keep the ingredients to one side. They must not get wet.  
Stir the mixture and feed it to the bear.  
That's all. A simple recipe on how to tame a bear :)
